Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the data updated?

Our system continuously monitors data sources and updates insights in real-time. You'll always have access to the latest market intelligence.

Can I export the data for external use?

Yes, you can export data in various formats from the Export Data section in the Tools menu. This feature is available for all paid plans.

How does the AI Assistant work?

Our AI analyzes the data in your current view and can provide summaries, competitive comparisons, and investment insights based on the available information.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, InsightPulse is optimized for web browsers. Our mobile-responsive design ensures a great experience on tablets and smartphones.
